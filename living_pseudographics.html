Rémi Georges is a sound artist, composer, and computer music designer based in
Lyon. His practice unfolds through interactive sound installations, algorithmic
and generative compositions, and improvised electronic performances.  Working
on technological archiving, he develops bespoke instruments and software to
craft unexpected performances and installations. His work is deeply rooted in
experimentation, always guided by a commitment to open-source principles.

Website:
remigeorges.fr

Adel Faure work is motivated by an experimental relationship with text-based
interfaces. Interested in the design of tools, their influence on creation and
culture, he bring back to life forms and practices from a computing era
considered obsolete, turn them into games, animation design and illustrations.
