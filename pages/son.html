ORG-MITRA, Vahan Soghomonian, 2024

ORG-MITRA, Vahan Soghomonian, 2024

ORG-MITRA is an autonomous sound installation, created by Vahan Soghomonian. Located a tunnel in the Monts d'Or region, it explores the musicality of a freeway, a transit network, a link between territories, and the tunnel, a place of passage, considered as a whisper of time.
My work as a composer, was to create the composition as well as the core of the installation, focusing on durability, interactivity and the sound properties of the installation. ORG-MITRA is a sound art and land art piece located in Saint-Germain-Nuelles and planned to be playing for 20 years.

ORG-RCHBRN, Vahan Soghomonian, 2023

ORG-RCHBRN, Vahan Soghomonian, 2024

ORG-RCHBRN, is an instrument, located in Drôme Provençale, built to capture and emit the sound and the generative song of the mountain. I worked on the core of the installation, weather sensors, human sensors, control panel integration. It's a 24/7 working installation generating differents sounds through the seasons... And it can be used as an instrument as well as a sound system.

Sound Creation made through PureData, Faust and Python, with the composers Fabien Ainardi and Irwin Barbé

Ralt144MI

Ralt144MI

Under the alias Ralt144MI, he creates hybrid electronic music that fuses the driving energy of techno, the visual and sonic aesthetics of the demoscene, and the intricacy of electroacoustic experimentation. His live-coded performances delve into the theme of technological archiving, weaving intricate rhythmic patterns with captivating visual elements to deliver deeply immersive audiovisual experiences.
This project has been developed and showcased with the support of esteemed institutions and festivals, including Fubar Festival (Zagreb), Athenor CNCM (Saint-Nazaire), Art Zoyd Studios (Valenciennes), Grame CNCM (Lyon), Arts Meets Radical Openness Festival (Linz), Amaze Festival (Berlin), Overkill Festival (Enschede)…

Art Zoyd, Anonymous sounds, 2024

Art Zoyd, Anonymous sounds, 2024

Rémi Georges' project at Art Zoyd is to create a performance piece resulting from a dive into the Studios' archives. A blend of Ralt144MI's aesthetic universe and the history of the site and its creations. The idea is to bring together, as a creative basis, sound, visual and technological materials, in an idea of archive work that is close to his heart, or in a certain form of technological archaeology. The aim is to create a piece in tune with the site, the spectator and his artistic convictions.
