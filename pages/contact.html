Rémi GEORGES

Computer Music Designer, Sound Technician,
Live-Coder Artist


Rémi Georges, based in Lyon, is a sound artist, composer, and computer music designer with a background in classical percussion and a Master’s in Computer Music Design. Merging traditional and contemporary technologies, he develops bespoke instruments and software to craft innovative performances and installations. His work focuses on sound installations, interactive generative compositions, and improvised electronic music, always guided by a commitment to open-source principles.
As a member of the Cookie Collective and TOPLAP, Rémi organizes events, leads workshops, and curates releases under his label Ordinateur dans la tête.
