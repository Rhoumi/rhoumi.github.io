Rémi GEORGES

Computer Music Designer, Sound Technician,
Live-Coder Artist


Rémi Georges is a computer music designer, sound artist and composer based in Lyon.
His practice unfolds through interactive sound installations, algorithmic and generative compositions, and improvised electronic performances.
Working on technological archiving, he develops bespoke instruments and software to craft unexpected performances and installations.
His work is deeply rooted in experimentation, always guided by a commitment to open-source principles.

As a member of the Cookie Collective and TOPLAP, Rémi organizes events, leads workshops, and curates releases under his label Ordinateur dans la tête.
